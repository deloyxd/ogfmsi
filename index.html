Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gym

Do you offer personal training?
Yes, there are freelance personal trainers who work independently at our gym. They are not employed or managed by us, and the gym does not take any commission or cut from their services. If you're interested, you're welcome to contact them directly to arrange sessions.
Are there any age restrictions?
Members must be at least 16 years old. Individuals between 16-18 years require parental consent. We offer student memberships with special rates for those in school.
What do I get access to with a daily or monthly pass?
Your daily or monthly pass includes full access to our gym floor. You are free to use any equipment until you leave the gym.
What are the gym pass pricing?
You can choose to pay daily pass for ₱70 (₱60 for student) or pay monthly pass for ₱950 (₱850 for student) .
