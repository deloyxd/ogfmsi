Fitworx Gym Customer Agreement

Customer Terms

Customers must complete and provide basic personal information at the Fitworx Gym registration.
Customers who are students are required to provide a valid student ID if they want to have the student discount in daily or monthly Fitworx Gym pass.
Customers has the freedom to use the whole gym floor until they left the gym. If they come back, they will need to pay for Fitworx Gym registration again.
Customers must use any Fitworx Gym equipment properly and professionally. After usage, equipments must be retured to their designated areas.
Customers must follow all the gym rules displayed on Fitworx Gym registration.

Trainer Terms

Fitworx Gym welcomes various trainers, but these trainers operate independently and are not associated with the gym. Any agreements made with trainers are between the trainer and their client.
Fitworx Gym will never request nor require the customers to pay for the trainers. It's up for the trainers if they want to get paid or not, by their clients.

Payment Terms

All payment are required prior to the delivery of any services and merchandise of Fitworx Gym. Advance payments are welcome.
Full payment is required in advance for all services and merchandise of Fitworx Gym. Partial payments will not be accepted.
